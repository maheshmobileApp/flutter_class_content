
Widgets are two types

1. stateless widget

. static widget
. They do not depend on any data changes or any behavior change.
. stateless widget do not have a state.
. they will be rendered once and will not update themseleves.

Ex: Text, icon ...

2. Statefull widget

dynamic widget
they can be updated during runtime based on user action or data change
. statefull widget have a internal state
. they can re-render if the input data changes or if widget's state changes
Checkbox, radio button ...
